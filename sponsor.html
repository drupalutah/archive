DrupalCamp Utah has grown exponentially over the last few years. We are optimistic that we'll have more than 150 this coming year, due largely to the growing popularity of Drupal and unique tracks offered for all levels of Drupal users.

However, the number of attendees isn't the only thing that makes this event possible. It's also the supporting organizations who donate money, time, and gifts. We are extremely appreciative for all the help we have received in previous years that has made this event what it is.

If you would like to help grow our Drupal community financially please choose a sponsorship level below and proceed to checkout. Or if you have any questions please contact an organizer.

We are operating under DrupalCon, Inc. and are considered a 501c3 not-for-profit event. We can accept payments by check or with a credit card (via PayPal). All money is handled by the Drupal Association.
