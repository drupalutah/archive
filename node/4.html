This year Drupal Camp Utah will be earlier in the year. We are optimistic that we'll have more than 50 this coming year for two reasons. First, we've added three distinct tracks designed to appeal to more audiences than the average developer. Second, we are hosting the event earlier in the year, during ski season rather than vacation season.

Note: 1/27/2015 -- We now estimate this year's attendance to be higher than previously estimated; we anticipate around 70 attendees.

Number of attendees isn't the only thing that makes this event possible, it's also the supporting organizations who donate money, time, and gifts. We are extremely appreciative for all the help we have received in previous years that has made this event what it is today.

If you would like to help grow our Drupal community financially please choose a sponsorship level below and proceed to checkout. Or if you have any questions please contact an organizer.

We are operating under DrupalCon, Inc. and are considered a 501c3 not-for-profit event. We can accept payments by check or with a credit card (via PayPal). All money is handled by the Drupal Association.
