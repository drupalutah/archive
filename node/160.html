Forena Reports and Easycharts

I just finished a large financial dashboard for a hospital. I used Forena Reports and Easychart to build them tabular and graphs dynamically from their financial database. There was a learning curve to Forena as well as some contributed code that I needed to get it complexly integrated into the Panopoly interface. I will bring you up the curve as fast as possible. I will review the 2 modules with their all their glory and warts.
