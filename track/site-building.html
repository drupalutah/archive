Commerce License and recurring billing

Submitted by Kazanir on Thu, 07/24/2014 - 02:56

Using the Commerce License and Commerce License Billing modules to sell access to resources and set up recurring subscriptions through the Drupal Commerce framework. We'll walk through the layout of the License and Billing modules and set up license and recurring products, talk about billing cycles, establish card on file and Dunning (delinquent user notification) support, and so on. Come to this if you want to sell access to files, roles, or group memberships using Drupal Commerce!

Introduction to Drupal

Submitted by logan_farr on Sat, 06/28/2014 - 14:26

What exactly is Drupal, and more importantly, why should you use it?

Drupal is a powerful content management framework, and figuring out how to get it up and running can be tricky. The administration bar can be confusing, and the way popular modules work is even more so.

In this sessions, I will share my experience and advice on beginning a Drupal website. This includes: navigating admin areas; best practice configurations, users and permissions, blocks, modules, and tips and tricks.
