Introduction to Drupal

Submitted by logan_farr on Jun 28, 2014.

What exactly is Drupal, and more importantly, why should you use it?

Drupal is a powerful content management framework, and figuring out how to get it up and running can be tricky. The administration bar can be confusing, and the way popular modules work is even more so.

In this sessions, I will share my experience and advice on beginning a Drupal website. This includes: navigating admin areas; best practice configurations, users and permissions, blocks, modules, and tips and tricks.
