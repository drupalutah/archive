Multilingual Site Setup and Management

Submitted by robertdbailey on Jun 17, 2014.

Multilingual was an afterthought in the design of Drupal 7. Luckily, there has been a significant effort within the community that now makes it possible and even simple. This session will discuss how to get a Drupal 7 site to be multilingual and how to manage it over time.

Topics:

Site preparations and dependencies
Decisions in D7 that affect upgrading to D8
Site translation: take 1!
Interface translation, compliments of the Drupal community (and you)
Translation of blocks, menus, views, taxonomy, and more
Coping with constantly changing content
Enlisting others to assist in the translation process
Translation workflow considerations and automation
Leveraging machine, community, and professional translation
