Welcome to the Utah Drupal Users Group! Utah is a state in the western part of the United States with some of the world's best ski slopes. Salt Lake City is the state capital. This group is intended to teach about Drupal, help with installation and collaborate in customization and development of new functionality (modules, themes and so on). All comments and questions are welcome. Please feel free to join this group. If you use irc you may want to join the #drupal-utah channel to discuss Drupal with local folks. Follow news about the Utah Drupal group on @utahdrupal or news specific to Drupalcamp Utah on @drupalcamputah. Meetups are announced as events in this page's feed. If you would like to start a meet-up and have it announced here, please let us know and we will add it. There is a meetup in the Salt Lake City area at CentralPoint's Sugarhouse office (21061 East 2100 South, Salt Lake City, UT 84106). The Salt Lake City meet-ups are generally on the fourth Thursday of each month. An event is posted for each meetup a week or two in advance to confirm that it is happening.

https://groups.drupal.org/utah
